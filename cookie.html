COOKIETO

Cookieto is a brand that embodies the perfect combination of deliciousness, fun, and quality. Our visual identity reflects these core values through a playful and vibrant design that highlights the unique texture and flavor of our cookies.

Color Palette: Cookieto's color palette features warm, appetizing hues that evoke a sense of comfort and joy. The primary colors include a rich brown that represents the perfectly baked cookies and the sweet brown sugar, paired with accents of cream and beige that symbolize the soft and chewy texture.

Typography: Cookieto's typography is bold and inviting, with a touch of whimsy that reflects the brand's playful spirit. The font is easy to read and has a hand-drawn quality that adds to the brand's approachable and friendly vibe.

Brand Mark: Cookieto's logo is simple and bold, making it easily recognizable and versatile across different mediums.

Overall, Cookieto's visual identity is a perfect blend of playfulness, warmth, and appetite appeal that invites people to indulge in the delicious taste and fun spirit of the brand.
